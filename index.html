The 6-Phase Source-to-Logic Lifecycle

A Complete Regulatory Compilation Loop

Phase 01 · Ingest

Establish Your Compliance IR Baseline

Compliance Compiler Network ingests your relevant regulatory frameworks UK-GDPR, Online Safety Act, DORA, NIS2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS and builds a comprehensive Compliance Intermediate Representation (IR) schema unique to your organisation.

The Ingest phase surfaces the hidden cost of unmanaged interpretation giving you a precise data-driven view of your Interpretation Gap before any strategy is deployed. Linguistic anchors (Actors, Obligations, Evidence Thresholds) are mapped and stored permanently.

Phase 02 · Compile

Convert Law into Machine-Executable Logic

The Source-to-Logic Compilation Engine automatically deconstructs each legal paragraph into multi-step technical workflows. A single UK-GDPR clause becomes a structured chain of executable controls, evidence requests, and audit checkpoints your IT team can actually run.

Compliance DNA Fingerprinting captures your organisation's unique legal-to-technical mapping style new compliance hires reach audit proficiency within 14 days vs. a historical average of 45 days.

Phase 03 · Monitor

Real-Time Compliance Delta Diagnostics

The CDD engine continuously monitors compiled control chains against live regulatory updates. When a new amendment is issued, the system identifies exactly which of your existing controls are impacted in real time, not weeks later.

Pilot deployments showed a 28% average increase in remediation speed within 8 weeks, and 18% reduction in control drift across 1,200 tested interactions.

Phase 04 · Validate

Auditor-in-the-Loop Governance

Led by Moulika Makkapati's expertise as an ISO 27001 Lead Auditor, the platform's outputs are validated against manual audit findings through the Human-in-the-Loop Governance Index satisfying the UK's strict governance requirements for automated decision-making.

While the compiler automates the logic, a human oversight layer remains for high-risk legal exceptions, ensuring compliance with the UK's Principles-Based AI Framework.

Phase 05 · Learn

Outcome-Learning Regulatory Memory

Every audit pass or failure is encoded back into the Compliance DNA Fingerprint. The platform builds a proprietary Outcome-Learning Regulatory Memory that maps successful audit interactions back to original compiled control structures.

This turns your historical compliance success into permanent organisational Compliance Equity an asset that survives staff turnover and protects institutional regulatory memory across generations of your compliance team.

Phase 06 · Scale

Federated Benchmarking & Global Expansion

The Federated Compliance Logic Network (FCLN) allows your organisation to benefit from anonymised Compliance DNA benchmarks across thousands of UK regulated firms improving predictive models without sharing sensitive business data.

Insights from high-compliance financial firms in London optimise control strategies for Manchester startups. Platform scales across EU and North American jurisdictions (NIST, SEC, CCPA) through framework-agnostic architecture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know
About Compliance Compiler Network

What is the "Interpretation Gap" and why does it matter?
The Interpretation Gap is the disconnect between raw legal regulatory text and the actual technical controls an organisation implements. Currently, compliance is dominated by manual human interpretation legal counsel must painstakingly read dense, fragmented texts. In 2024 alone, this gap caused $14 billion in global non-compliance fines. Compliance Compiler Network eliminates this by converting law directly into machine-executable logic the world's first Source-to-Logic compilation platform.
How is Compliance Compiler Network different from GRC tools like OneTrust or Vanta?
Traditional GRC tools like OneTrust are static repositories "digital filing cabinets" that store policies but rely entirely on manual human interpretation to map legal text to controls. Vanta provides automated evidence collection for pre-defined frameworks like SOC2, but cannot "compile" new or custom legal text. Compliance Compiler Network is the only platform with a Source-to-Logic compilation engine turning raw regulatory text into machine-executable compliance logic automatically, with a 92% Quiet Failure detection accuracy proven in R&D.
What is Compliance DNA and why should I protect it?
Compliance DNA is the proprietary legal-to-technical mapping logic built up by your compliance team over years of audits, regulatory interactions, and framework management. When a Chief Compliance Officer or senior GRC professional leaves, this knowledge walks out the door creating "Interpretation Loss." Compliance Compiler Network captures and encodes this logic permanently into the platform, so the organisation owns it as Compliance Equity regardless of staff turnover. Pilot firms found new junior compliance analysts performing at specialist level within just one week.
Which regulatory frameworks does the platform support?
Compliance Compiler Network supports UK-GDPR, Online Safety Act (OSA), DORA, NIS2, ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI DSS, HIPAA, NIST CSF, and the UK Principles-Based AI Framework. The platform is framework-agnostic by design built to sit above any underlying regulatory framework, ensuring businesses aren't locked into a single compliance standard as they expand geographically into EU and North American markets (NIST, SEC, CCPA).
How quickly can we get started and see results?
Pilot deployments have shown that participating firms see a 28% average increase in remediation speed within the first 8 weeks. New compliance hires reach audit proficiency within 14 days of system activation (vs. a historical average of 45 days). Cloud and GRC stack integrations (AWS/Azure, ServiceNow, Jira) reduce the standard onboarding timeline from weeks to hours through our public API layer.
Is the platform compliant with UK data privacy requirements?
Yes. The platform is fully compliant with the UK's Principles-Based AI Framework (DSIT) and ICO guidance on AI and Data Protection (UK GDPR). Secure on-premise Compliance IR Nodes ensure sensitive organisational logic is captured without compromising data privacy standards. The Federated Compliance Logic Network (FCLN) uses fully anonymised benchmarking data no individual firm's sensitive compliance logic is ever shared with any other organisation.
What does "Compliance Equity" mean for my business?
Compliance Equity is the concept of converting transient employee legal expertise into permanent organisational knowledge assets. Instead of compliance being a variable cost that fluctuates with staff turnover, it becomes a fixed, growing asset. Compliance Compiler Network calculates your specific Compliance Equity score in real time, showing senior leadership exactly how compiled regulatory logic reduces risk-to-output ratios and contributes to long-term institutional resilience aligning with the UK's 2025 Smart Data and Digital Growth goals.
Can the platform handle overlapping and conflicting regulatory frameworks?
Yes this is one of our core innovations. The Jurisdiction-Aware Delta Compilation engine specifically handles "regulatory collision" scenarios where a single business process is subject to three or four overlapping legal frameworks (e.g., DORA and UK Operational Resilience rules simultaneously). The system fingerprints overlapping requirements, enabling your firm to implement a single technical control that satisfies multiple jurisdictions simultaneously eliminating the redundancy loss that occurs in manual mapping.
